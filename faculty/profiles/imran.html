Professional Experience:

With over 20 years of distinguished experience in Computer Engineering education, Prof. Imran Ali Mirza has been a cornerstone of academic excellence at Don Bosco Institute of Technology (DBIT). His extensive tenure includes 7 years as Head of Department and 5 years as Dean of Administration, where he demonstrated exceptional leadership in curriculum design, academic administration, and student mentorship. His comprehensive expertise spans programming methodologies, database management systems, and innovative pedagogical approaches that have shaped the academic journey of countless engineering students.
